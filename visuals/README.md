Mamaearth Onion Hair Oil - Product README
🌿 Product Name
Mamaearth Onion Hair Oil
📖 Description
Mamaearth Onion Hair Oil is a natural hair care product designed to reduce hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. Enriched with Onion Oil, Redensyl, and various plant-based oils, it nourishes the scalp and strengthens hair from the roots.
✨ Key Features
Helps reduce hair fall
Promotes healthy hair growth
Strengthens weak and damaged hair
Nourishes the scalp deeply
Made with natural ingredients
Suitable for all hair types
🌱 Ingredients
Onion Oil – Strengthens hair and reduces hair fall.
Redensyl – Supports hair growth and improves hair density.
Almond Oil – Nourishes and moisturizes the hair.
Castor Oil – Helps strengthen hair roots.
Bhringraj Oil – Traditionally used for healthy hair growth.
💡 Benefits
Reduces hair fall
Improves hair texture
Makes hair soft and shiny
Nourishes the scalp
Encourages thicker and healthier hair
🧴 How to Use
Take an adequate amount of oil.
Apply directly to the scalp using fingertips.
Massage gently in circular motions for 5–10 minutes.
Leave it on for a few hours or overnight.
Wash off with a mild shampoo.
⚠️ Precautions
For external use only.
Avoid contact with eyes.
Store in a cool and dry place.
Keep out of reach of children.
